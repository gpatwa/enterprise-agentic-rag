Tagline: Ask. Verify. Act. · The unified answer-and-action layer for enterprise data. Logo, wordmark, favicon, and usage guidelines below.

01 · The Mark

A compass needle that breaks the ring.

The needle (diagonal arrow) sits inside an open ring. The ring's gap doubles as the implicit "C" of Compass. Together: navigation + grounded direction + brand initial.

02 · Wordmark

Mark + lowercase wordmark.

Inter Display, weight 600, letter-spacing -0.02em, lowercase. Lowercase signals approachable, not corporate. Mark to the left, 16px gap.

03 · Favicon & App Icon

Simplified for tiny sizes.

At 16px and below, the ring becomes a hint. The needle does the heavy lifting. Rounded square (8px corner) with brand-dark fill. Maskable for Android home-screen.

06 · Usage rules

Do & Don't.

✓ DO
  • · Use minimum 24px height for the wordmark
  • · Maintain clear-space equal to the ring width on all sides
  • · Use brand gradient on dark backgrounds
  • · Pair the lockup left of headline copy
  • · Use favicon for sub-32px contexts
✗ DON'T
  • · Stretch, skew, or rotate the mark
  • · Place on busy photo backgrounds without scrim
  • · Reverse colors of the ring vs needle
  • · Combine with another emoji or icon
  • · Use marketing red/green/orange for the mark
